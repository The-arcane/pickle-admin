# Platform Features by Role

This document outlines the features available to each user role within the Lumen platform.

---

## 1. Super Admin

The Super Admin has the highest level of access and can manage the entire platform.

### Platform Management
- **Dashboard**: A high-level overview of all platform-wide activity, including total organizations, users, bookings, and more.
- **Living Spaces**: Create and manage residential-type organizations.
- **Schools**: Create and manage education-type organizations.
- **Hospitality**: Create and manage hospitality-type organizations.
- **Arena**: Create and manage public arena/sports complex organizations.

### User Management
- **Users**: View and manage all users across all organizations.
- **Admins**: Create and manage Admin-level users for various organizations.
- **Sales**: Create and manage Sales team members.
- **Invitations**: Send and manage invitations for users to join organizations.

### Operational Data
- **Courts**: View and manage all courts across all organizations.
- **Bookings**: View and manage all court bookings across the platform.
- **Events**: View and manage all events across the platform.

### Financials
- **Transactions**: Monitor all financial transactions platform-wide.
- **Payments**: Manage payouts to organizations.
- **Invoices**: View all invoices generated across the platform.

### Support
- **Tickets**: View and manage support tickets from all organizations.
- **Chats**: Review transcripts of live support chats.

---

## 2. Living Space Admin

This role manages a specific residential community (Living Space).

- **Dashboard**: A snapshot of the Living Space's activity, including recent bookings and upcoming events.
- **Approvals**: Approve or reject requests from users to join the Living Space.
- **Invitations**: Invite new residents via CSV upload.
- **Courts**: Add, edit, and manage court details, availability, and rules.
- **Bookings**: Create and manage court and event bookings for residents.
- **Events**: Create and manage community events.
- **Channels**: Manage real-time communication channels for the community.
- **Users**: View all users associated with the Living Space.
- **Employees**: Add and manage employees for the Living Space.
- **Coaches**: Add and manage coaches, including their specializations and pricing.
- **Living Space Profile**: Manage the organization's structure (buildings, wings, flats).
- **Profile & Settings**: Manage personal admin profile and theme settings.

---

## 3. Arena Admin

This role manages a public-facing sports facility or arena.

- **Dashboard**: An overview of the arena's activities, including key stats and quick actions.
- **Bookings**: Manage court and event bookings for arena members and the public.
- **Events**: Create and manage public or private events and tournaments.
- **Members**: View all registered members of the arena.
- **Courts**: Add, edit, and manage court details, availability, pricing, and visibility (public/private).
- **Channels**: Manage real-time communication channels for the arena.
- **Coaches**: Add and manage coaches available at the arena.
- **Financials**:
    - **Invoices**: View and manage invoices.
    - **Transactions**: Review all financial transactions.
    - **Transfers**: Manage payouts to the arena's bank account.
    - **Bank Details**: Configure the bank account for receiving payouts.
- **Support**:
    - **Contact Us**: Access support contact information.
    - **Raise a Ticket**: Submit and track support requests.
- **Profile & Settings**: Manage personal admin profile and theme settings.

---

## 4. Education Admin

This role is tailored for managing a school or educational institution's sports activities.

- **Dashboard**: An overview of school activities, including attendance, student counts, and announcements.
- **Academics**:
    - **Attendance**: Create sessions and mark student attendance.
    - **Skills**: Assess and track player skill progression.
    - **Users**: Manage school administrative users.
    - **Parents**: Manage and invite parents/guardians.
- **Activities**:
    - **Events**: Manage school tournaments, matches, and other events.
    - **Season**: Manage the current season's leaderboard.
    - **Media**: Manage photos and videos from events.
    - **Calendar**: View a full schedule of classes and events.
- **Administration**:
    - **Notifications**: Send automated or manual notifications for events.
    - **Communication**: Manage the faculty and coach directory.
    - **Reports**: View analytics on attendance and participation.
    - **Emergency Alerts**: Send urgent alerts to parents and faculty.
    - **Inventory**: Track and manage school sports equipment.
    - **Resources**: Share training materials and resources.
    - **Sponsors**: Manage and track sponsorships.
    - **Health & Safety**: Log injuries and monitor student health records.
- **Settings**: Manage personal admin profile and theme settings.

---

## 5. Hospitality Admin

This role manages hospitality services and packages.

- **Dashboard**: An overview of active packages and total bookings.
- **Packages**: Create, edit, and manage hospitality packages (e.g., corporate outings, party bookings).
- **Bookings**: View and manage all package bookings.
- **Settings**: Manage personal admin profile and theme settings.

---

## 6. Sales

This role focuses on onboarding new organizations onto the platform.

- **Dashboard**: A view of inactive organizations that need to be onboarded.
- **Living Spaces**: View, edit, and activate new Living Space organizations. Includes the ability to edit the organization's public-facing webpage.

---

## 7. Employee

This is a limited-access role for on-ground staff at a facility.

- **Dashboard**: A simple dashboard with quick access to essential tools.
- **Scan QR**: Scan user QR codes to check in and verify bookings for courts and events.
- **Bookings**: View all court bookings for the facility.
- **Events**: View all scheduled events for the facility.
